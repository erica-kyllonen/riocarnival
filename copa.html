The Copacabana

PALACE BALL

One of the highlights of the Rio Carnival is the Copa Ball at the iconic Copacabana Palace Hotel, a symbol of luxury and sophistication in Rio de Janeiro. This eagerly anticipated event is widely regarded as the grandest and most opulent occasion of the entire Carnival season, drawing in a glittering array of high-profile guests from around the globe. The ballroom transforms into a dazzling spectacle of color, music, and fashion, where attendees don elaborate costumes, designer gowns, and custom-tailored tuxedos that reflect the grandeur of the celebration. The evening is an extravagant fusion of Rio’s vibrant culture and world-class elegance, with a carefully curated program that includes live samba performances, orchestral music, and gourmet cuisine prepared by renowned chefs. Celebrities, socialites, and dignitaries mingle under the shimmering chandeliers, making it a hotspot for media coverage and social buzz. The energy in the room is electric, as the samba rhythms fill the air, and guests dance late into the night, embodying the true spirit of Carnival—an exuberant celebration of life, music, and unity. For many, the Copa Ball is not just a party; it is a once-in-a-lifetime experience that showcases the magic and allure of Rio Carnival at its finest.

Sensational Costumes for an Enchanting Evening
The Copa Ball at the Copacabana Palace is without doubt the most elite event of Rio. Photographers from the press and other media are kept busy with each celebrity trying to outdo the other while posing in their gorgeous gowns and costumes. Previous Copa Balls have attracted such names as Paris Hilton and Gerald Butler along with a host of South American film stars and socialites. It is the place to see and be seen, and few would miss the opportunity to mingle with big international names in such a high-status setting.
The Iconic Copacabana Palace
Generations of movie stars and celebrities has enjoyed the Copacabana Palace Hotel and it’s fine amenities. The Palace Hotel has been a cultural symbol of Rio for over 100 years, and the Carnival Ball decorates it’s halls every year with elaborate changing themes. Guests are encouraged to consider the theme of the Ball when choosing their costumes. The Copacabana Palace Ball is the most talked about Carnival ball in Rio de Janeiro. All tickets include an open bar and buffet all night from the exquisite dining and bar tending from the Copacabana Palace.

Ticket Details
Important information when buying your ticket to this event:

BAILE DE COPA TICKETS
All tickets entitle you to circulate in all areas of the Ball.  Differences you should know about:

INDIVIDUAL GOLDEN ROOM
Individual tickets with the right to a seat at one of the tables in the main hall. This is the room where the night boils, with renowned DJs and Brazilian musicians animating the room all night long, and a dance floor for you to enjoy yourself. 

INDIVIDUAL NOBLE ROOM
Individual tickets with the right to a seat at one of the tables in the hall. This hall, which gives access to the Golden Room, is a lounge to sit, eat and chat with friends in peace.

STANDING TICKETS
Individual tickets that entitle you to circulate through all the halls, and make the most of the entire party, but without a seat at one of the tables.
