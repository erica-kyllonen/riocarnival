TICKET INFO

At the heart of the Rio Carnival lies the dazzling Samba Parade, a spectacular celebration of Brazil's culture and creativity. Hosted in the iconic Sambadrome Marquês de Sapucaí, a structure designed by famed architect Oscar Niemeyer, this event showcases the fierce competition between Rio's top samba schools. Performers parade down the long avenue, flanked by enthusiastic crowds in tiered stands, as they bring intricate floats, stunning costumes, and high-energy samba routines to life. Each school tells a unique story through their performance, blending elements of Brazilian history, folklore, and contemporary themes in a breathtaking display of artistry and community spirit.

The Sambadrome offers a variety of ticket packages to suit all budgets, from general admission for those seeking a lively, immersive experience to luxury suites offering VIP amenities such as catered dining and open bars. Regardless of the seating option, attendees are treated to an unforgettable night of music, movement, and color. The energy of the performers and the rhythm of samba music electrify the air, making the Samba Parade a must-see event and the pinnacle of the Rio Carnival experience.
Grandstands
Standing Room Only - Exciting Atmosphere - Best Price
Most cheerful places. The seats are large concrete steps with a great view of the parade. Most affordable ticket package from the Sambadrome, Grandstands seats are not numbered or assigned, except for Sector 9, the Star Sector, which provides more comfort and space, and a better structure overall.
Allocated Chairs
Seated Section - Guaranteed Chair - End of Parade Route
Allocated Chairs are assigned seats, available only in Sector 12 of the Sambadrome. The sector is organized in an open-air theatre of chairs facing the parade. Ideal for those who want to secure an assigned numbered chair without paying too much.
Front Box
Ground Level - Private Boxes - Closest Tickets to Parade
The Front Box is known as the best location to watch the parades at the Rio Sambadrome, located at ground level, near the track, in four rows of boxes from A to D, with a capacity for six people in each box. They offer a less crowded experience and Sector 9 is recommended for its better structure and proximity to transfer access. The front box areas are the least crowded of the Sambadrome.
Folia Tropical
VIP Lounge and Access - All-Inclusive Buffet and Bar
The best lounge at the venue, Folia is ideal for party people who want to enjoy the parade and have fun at the same time. Located at Sector 8, in front of the drum section and the judges' booth, offers an open bar, buffet, DJs, shows, and round-trip transfers to the Sambadrome from Leblon and Barra da Tijuca. Capacity of 800 people.
